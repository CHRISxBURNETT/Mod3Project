Hardware

For our Module III project I’m going to show you guys how to proper install your hard drive, RAM, expansion slots, and amongst other thing. It is important to know how to properly install your hardware because you want to make sure you not damaging the hardware by trying to shove it in. Also you want to want to avoid electrostatic discharge (ESD) when dealing with hardware. ESD can damage the equipment’s internal components.

  1. 1.Turn off the computer and unplug the wires connected to the tower.
  2. 2.Take the screws on the back of the tower, remove the panel, then wear an anti-static wrist strap.
  3. 3.Grab the motherboard, slide it into the case, and screw it in.
  4. 4.Once the motherboard is safely secure start to insert your RAM. The RAM slots are usually located at the top right of the motherboard.
  5. 5.Then go ahead and place the CPU into the socket. The CPU socket is square shaped on the motherboard
  6. 6.Next is to install your video, sound, network, and graphic cards on the motherboard. These go into the PCI Express slots are the motherboard.
  7. 7.After th cards are installed, then you can slowly install the hard drive into the hard drive bay. The hard drive bay is usually located at the front of the tower.
  8. 8.If you have a DVD drive, you can install it in the bay at the top (right or left depending on the panel that was open.
  9. 9.Finally, after installing the hardware into the computer, go ahead and start connecting the wires to the hardware.
  10. 10.Once the wires are connected, put the panel back on. After screwing the screws back into the panel, plug in the power supply. The computer should turn on and functioning properly.
