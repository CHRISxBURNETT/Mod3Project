Interviews

It is really important to make good eye contact, shake hands firmly, arrive 10-15 mins early if do all this things and more it shows confidence. What you shouldn’t do is chew gum, tell jokes, answer phone calls, and lie about your resume it doesn’t help your professional brand.

    Do’s
  1. 1.Do your research and know the type of job interview you will encounter
  2. 2.Plan to arrive 10-15 minutes early before you schedule
  3. 3.Greet the receptionist or assistance with courtesy and respect
  4. 4.Bring extra resumes to the interview
  5. 5.Greet the interviewer(s) by title (Ms., Mr., Dr.) and last name if you are sure of the pronunciation. (If you’re not sure, do ask the receptionist about the pronunciation before going into the interview.
  6. 6.Shake hands firmly
  7. 7.Make good eye contact
  8. 8.Show enthusiasm in the position and the company
  9. 9.Avoid using poor language, slang, and pause words (such as “like,” “uh,” and “um”)
  10. 10.Have a high confidence and energy level, but don’t be overly aggressive

    11. Don’ts
  11. 1. Don’t chew gum during an interview
  12. 2. Don’t rely on your application or resume to do the selling for you
  13. 3. Don’t tell jokes during the interview
  14. 4. Don’t be soft-spoken. Have a forceful voice projects confidence
  15. 5. Don’t say anything negative about former colleagues, supervisors, or employers
  16. 6. Don’t answer cell-phone calls during the interview
  17. 7. Don’t bring up or discuss personal issues or family problems
  18. 8. Don’t answer questions with a simple “yes” or “no”. Explain wherever possible
  19. 9. Don’t ever lie
  20. 10. Don’t smoke, even if the interviewer does and offers you a cigarette. And don’t smoke beforehand so that you smell like smoke. Do brush your teeth, use mouthwash, or have a breath mint before the interview
